Rhythm is categorized mainly into binary and ternary rhythms.

Binary rhythms are based on groupings of two.

  • Characteristics: Grouped in twos, common in pop and rock music.
  • Examples:
    • 4/4 Time
    • 2/4 Time
    • 6/8 Time (feels binary)


Ternary rhythms are based on groupings of three.

  • Characteristics: Grouped in threes, common in waltz and jazz.
  • Examples:
    • 3/4 Time
    • 6/8 Time (feels ternary)
    • 9/8 Time
