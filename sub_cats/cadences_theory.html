There are several cadences using different degrees of scales:

  • Authentic / Perfect Cadence: Degrees V then I
  • Plagal Cadence: Degrees IV then I
  • Interrupted / Deceptive Cadence: Degrees V then VI
  • Imperfect / Half Cadence: Degrees I or II or IV then V
