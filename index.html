This website is a comprehensive resource for students and enthusiasts eager to deepen their understanding of music theory. The site is divided into several sections, each dedicated to a key aspect of music theory: intervals, chords, cadences, scales, and rhythms. Each section includes exercises designed to reinforce theoretical concepts and provide practical experience.

  1. Intervals
    • Theory: Overview of intervals, including harmonic and melodic types.
    • Exercises: Practice identifying and constructing intervals by ear and sight.
  2. Chords
    • Theory: Explanation of triads, seventh chords, inversions, and extended chords.
    • Exercises: Identify various chord types.
  3. Cadences
    • Theory: Overview of perfect, imperfect, plagal, and deceptive cadences.
    • Exercises: Identify and create cadences in different musical contexts.
  4. Scales
    • Theory: Basics of major, minor, and modal scales.
    • Exercises: Identify and construct scales on a virtual keyboard or staff.
  5. Rhythms
    • Theory: Explanation of note values, time signatures, and rhythmic patterns.
    • Exercises: Practice recognizing, transcribing, and creating rhythms.
